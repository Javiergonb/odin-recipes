Strawberry Smoothie

Descripton

A smoothie is a thick beverage served cold that's most commonly made with a mixture of fruits and vegetables (In this case strawberry). It usually starts with a liquid base like water, dairy, or plant-based milk, and all the ingredients are pureed in a high-speed blender for smoothies

Ingredients

  • 8 strawberries, hulled
  • 1/2 cup skim milk
  • Â½ cup plain yogurt
  • 3 tablespoons white sugar
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 6 cubes ice, crushed

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Combine strawberries, milk, yogurt, sugar, and vanilla in a blender. Add ice and blend until smooth and creamy.
  3. Pour into glasses and serve immediately. Enjoy!
